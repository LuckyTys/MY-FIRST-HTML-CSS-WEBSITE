UMWANZURO

Sogokuru Sebigana yatanze iminani ku bana yabyaye muribo harimo na Mama Nyirantashoboka amuha ubutaka buherereye mu mudugudu wa Cyeru akagari ka Kanazi Umurenge wa Nyamata akarere ka Bugesera nk’ uko byemezwa n’ abatangabuhamya barimo abahawe iminani bavukana na mama aribo Nsengiyumva Francois, Ndagijimana Leonard Reberaho Michel na Musabyimana Emelita.

Nyuma yibyo Mama Nyirantashoboka yahise yubako inzu ariko amazemo iminsi mike avamo ajya kuba ku mugabo wa kabiri. Ikibazo cyaje kuvuka ubwo Mukuru wanjye yajyaga kubaruza ubwo butaka bwa Mama hanyuma Mukantagara aritambika ashyirishamo amakimbirane aho dukuriye nibwo twatangije gukurikirana umunani wa mama Nyirantashoboka aribwo twamenyeko Mukantagara ashaka kuwuturiganya hanyuma tuwuregera mu nzego z’ ibanze hose bemeza muri raporo yok u wa 11/09/2019 na 10/09/2023 ko ubutaka bwa Mama kubugiraho uburenganzira kubera ko Mukantagara awufite mu buryo bunyuranyije n’ amategeko.

Iki kibazo twakiregeye inzego z’ abunzi b’ Umurenge ndetse no mu kagari baca urubanza ariko nyuma yo gukora igenagaciro ry’ ikiburanwa dusanga kitari mu bubasha bw’ abunzi akaba ariyo mpamvu ngannye urukiko rw’ ibanze rwa Nyamata kugira ngo rundenganure.
